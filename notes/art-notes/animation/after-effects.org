#+title: After Effects: Notes
* Animation Playback
** Looping -
1. To loop animation first create a opening key and closing key on the timeline of whatever you want to loop 
2. Hold down u and click the timer button 
3. Now you can put in expressions
4. type in loopOut(); or loopOut(“cycle”); this will cycle through your keys for the duration of the scene
make sure to drag it out all the way
